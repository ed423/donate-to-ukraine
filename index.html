#StandWithUkraine

Military Efforts

Support for the Ukrainian armed forces, as well as organizations directly aimed at strengthening the Ukrainian resistance. Your donations will contribute directly to the Ukrainian military, and to organizations such as 7,62 project, a group of volunteers who provide Ukrainians with real-time updates on Russian troop movement on the Ukrainian border.

  • Armed Forces of Ukraine

Humanitarian Aid

Support for people directly affected by the conflict in Ukraine. Your donations will contribute towards short-term resources to help those in danger seek shelter, and provide essential resources such as food and water to refugees.

  • ICRC (International Committee of the Red Cross)

  • Doctors Without Borders

  • Polish Humanitarian Action

  • Polish Center for International Aid

Journalism

Supporting free press. Your donations will help journalists and news organizations to continue to provide vital and accurate information to those affected by the war, and to spread the word to those who are misinformed or uninformed about the crisis.

  • Kyiv Independent (GoFundMe)

This is a website created by volunteers to simplify the process of looking for credible links to donate to the support of those affected by the war in Ukraine.
